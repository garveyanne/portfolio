About Anne Garvey

Hello there!
I am a web designer based in Tokyo, and I am truly passionate about creating cohesive and visually appealing websites.

In my previous career, I spent a decade as an educator, but I felt a strong desire for a change in my path. I've always had a flair for creativity and enjoyed supporting others from behind the scenes. So, I decided to embark on a journey of self-study in Web Design and later joined an intensive Coding Bootcamp here in Tokyo to further enhance my skills.
