About Anne Garvey

Hello there!
I am a web designer based in Tokyo, and I am truly passionate about creating cohesive and visually appealing websites.

In my previous career, I spent a decade as an educator, but I felt a strong desire for a change in my path. I've always had a flair for creativity and enjoyed supporting others from behind the scenes. So, I decided to embark on a journey of self-study in Web Design and later joined an intensive Coding Bootcamp here in Tokyo to further enhance my skills. Now, I am continuing to expand my knowledge through personal studies while also working as a freelancer, crafting websites for various clients!

Apart from Web Design, I find great joy in writing. Currently, I am working on my own novel, which is a work in progress. I am taking my time to ensure it's something special.

I would love to connect with you and collaborate on projects together! If you're interested in getting in touch, please feel free to do so. Thank you for taking the time to read about me and my journey!
